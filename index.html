About Me

Welcome! Im your host Aidan Domenis and here's what you need to know about me. I'm currently a student enrolled in the Interactive Media developmant program at Confederation College, in Thunder Bay, Ontario. I have a passion for design and creating unique visual experiences. The majority of the work I do is based out of programs from the adobe suite, besides programs like blender and various coding platforms. Outside of the design world, I am a drummer in a band called Visions of Doyle, and music has a major influence on my life. My admiration of 60's music has lead me to become a super fan of 60's pop culture and fashion. This has influnced me to the point where I took up sewing in attempt to recreate some of my favourite looks from that era. Although books are cool as heck, I prefer visual learning strategies or hands on work. I like to believe that if I tell myself I can create what I envision and I truly believe in myself, there is absolutely no reason why I cannot make it happen. For some examples of my work, scroll down to my portfolio. Enjoy!
